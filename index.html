Qashupp Privacy Policy

At Qashupp, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("Qashupp" or the "App") and associated services (collectively, "Services").

By using Qashupp, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our Services.

  • a. Personal Information
    When you register or use Qashupp, we may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, and contact information.

    We collect information about your interactions with the App, such as ad views, coins earned, and redemption activities.
  • c. Device Information
    We may collect data related to your device, including IP address, device type, operating system, and settings.

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and personalize the Services.
  • Track ad views and rewards earned.

  • Improve user experience and enhance functionality.

  • Analyze usage and ad interactions to optimize the App’s performance.

We may share your information in the following situations:

Qashupp may contain advertisements from third parties. These advertisements may collect and use information about your device and activity within the App. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services, and we recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.
